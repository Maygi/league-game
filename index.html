Choose the difficulty

W - Jump
A/D - Move
Y -> Autoattack chain
U -> Quick strong attack
A/D + U -> Side strong attack chain
S + U -> Side dash with invuln frame
Spacebar -> Speed up text

Consecutive autoattacks or side
strong attacks will result in higher
damage numbers. Breaking the chain
or waiting too long before finishing
chain will reset the chain.

While using your dash, you are 
completely invulnerable to all
attacks. Be aware though that all
strong attacks and dashes consume
stamina.

Your quick strong attack and dash
can be used to animation cancel
your other abilities.

Enemy attacks take priority over
your own. If Rek'sai swings while
you attack, you will get hurt while
Rek'sai doesn't.
